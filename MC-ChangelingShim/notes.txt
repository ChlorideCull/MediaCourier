RTMP URL now in the format of rtmp://<hostname>/live/<key>
This ends up at the FCGI server as "name" inside the QUERY_STRING of the parameters

First argument of MediaCourier-ChangelingShim can be three modes
* fcgi - starts an FastCGI authenticator
* start-publish - connects to, and forwards an incoming stream to their defined remotes
* finalize-recording - convert saved flv in the background to a more optimized format for storing VODs
